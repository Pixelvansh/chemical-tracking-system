How ChemTrack Works

A 5-step chain of custody that makes chemical diversion physically impossible — whether for drugs, explosives, or illegal exports.

1
📋

Register & Get URN

Company registers on portal. NCB verifies documents and issues Unique Registration Number (URN). No URN = No chemical.

Register Your Firm →
2
✅

NCB Approves Purchase

Before buying raw material, manufacturer requests NCB approval. Quantity, supplier, and purpose verified in real-time.

3
🔲

Hierarchical QR Generated

Every drum gets a unique QR code. When split between parties, parent QR divides into child QRs — all linked and tracked on blockchain.

4
📍

GPS + Scan at Every Step

Every handover requires mandatory QR scan. GPS captured automatically. Quantity mismatch = instant NCB alert with exact location.

Innovation

3 Unique Features

Capabilities that no existing portal in India — or the world — has for precursor chemical tracking.

World First
🔲

Hierarchical QR System

When 500kg chemical splits between multiple parties, the Master QR divides into child QRs — all linked in a parent-child chain. Every split tracked. Every gram accounted for. 500kg splits into 200+250 but only 400 confirmed — 100kg missing = instant NCB alert.

Unique to ChemTrack
⚖️

Mass Balance Checker

Hospital received 1000kg chemical. Formula calculates 10,000 tablets should have been produced. Actual: 5,000 tablets. 500kg unaccounted = drug/explosive production suspected = NCB alert. No portal anywhere in the world has this feature.

Tamper Proof
🔗

Blockchain Audit Trail

Every transaction recorded on immutable blockchain. No one — manufacturer, distributor, or NCB officer — can alter any record. Complete chain of custody from factory to end use. Forever.

Existing System vs ChemTrack

India has 3 government portals for chemical regulation — but none track physical movement of a single drum.

❌ Without ChemTrack — What Happens

  • Chemicals diverted to drug, bomb & cartel networks
  • Manufacturer self-reports quarterly — easily faked
  • No real-time GPS on any chemical movement
  • No QR codes on drums or containers
  • No mandatory buyer confirmation system
  • No AI anomaly or overconsumption detection
  • No mass balance — missing chemical undetected
  • Records can be altered — no blockchain
  • India blacklisted internationally for chemical exports
