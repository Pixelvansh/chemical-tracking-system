NCB & DGFT Compliance Rules for Exporters

✅Valid IEC (Import Export Code) mandatory for all exports
✅DGFT No-Objection Certificate required for each export batch
✅QR code must be physically attached to every export shipment
✅Customs declaration must match QR-encoded chemical quantity
✅GPS capture mandatory at port of export
⚠️Annual export report must be submitted to NCB by March 31
⚠️Any export to flagged countries requires special NCB clearance
❗Quantity mismatch ≥15% triggers immediate NCB + Customs alert
